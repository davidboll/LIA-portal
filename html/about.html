Who are we?

Welcome to CM Educations "Recruitment of the future" - a gamechanging platform that changes the landscape of matching interns with internships and creates meaningful relationships.

We believe in the power of changing the future through investments in the next generation of developers. Trough "Recruitment of the future" we are creating ways for our students to prosper and for organizations to find the person that matches perfectly to their specific needs.

We look forward to being your partner in this exiting journey and to be a part of shaping the future through the next generation.
